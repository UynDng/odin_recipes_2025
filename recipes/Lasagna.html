Italian Lasagna

Home

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It’s baked until golden and bubbly, making it the perfect comfort food.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion and garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian herbs
